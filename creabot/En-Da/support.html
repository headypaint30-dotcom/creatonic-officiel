About the Minecraft® Server

The Backroom SMP Minecraft® server is an RP server where people enjoy hanging out and having fun with friends.

Almost anything is allowed (with some rules, of course):

Griefing is prohibited.

Raiding bases is prohibited.

Entering private structures is prohibited.

X-Ray and any form of cheating are prohibited.

PvP in peaceful zones is strictly prohibited.

For "OP" players, excessive use of /give is prohibited.

Rules may be added, so for safety, check the website and server regularly.
